Our Key Initiatives

Hands-On Workshops

We partner with schools and community centers to deliver engaging, single-session workshops. These labs, from creating glow-in-the-dark paint to building simple circuits, are designed to make complex scientific concepts accessible and fun for elementary and middle school students.

Extended In-Class Series

For a deeper dive, we offer 4-10 week series that integrate into the school curriculum. These programs allow students to explore topics like biology and environmental science more thoroughly, building on their knowledge week after week and culminating in a final project.

Community Science Nights

Our large-scale Science Nights bring entire communities together for an evening of scientific discovery. We set up multiple experiment stations, allowing families to explore different STEM fields at their own pace in a lively, festival-like atmosphere.
