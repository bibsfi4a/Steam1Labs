Our Programs

Who We Are

STEAMLabs International is a student-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit bringing free, hands-on science to underserved elementary and middle school students. We partner with schools, libraries, and after-school programs to spark curiosity through short, high-impact labs that kids remember.

What a Session Looks Like

Our sessions typically run about one hour. Students begin with a quick icebreaker to meet their mentors, then watch a short introduction and live demonstration of the experiment. Next, they spend 25-30 minutes completing the lab themselves with guidance from mentors. To close, we explain the science behind the activity, and every student leaves with a handout and a take-home experiment to extend the learning at home.

Sample Experiments

Most of our programs are chemistry-focused, featuring engaging labs such as:

  • Glow-in-the-Dark Paint
  • Instant Snow
  • Elephant Toothpaste
  • Color-Changing Spray Art
  • DIY Paint

For 4-10 week in-class series, we also offer biology-centered labs, giving students a deeper dive into life sciences.

How It Works

  • All materials provided: We supply experiment kits at no cost, pre-packed and labeled per table with clear safety guidance.
  • Classroom size: Sessions typically host 25-30 students (up to 35). Larger venues can scale up to 100 students.
  • Trained mentors: Each session is supported by 3-5 high school volunteers per class (about 1 mentor for every 5-6 students). For bigger events, we can bring up to 20 trained mentors to ensure every child gets hands-on guidance.

Customization

Every program is flexible and can be adapted to your needs. Schools and teachers can:

  • Choose labs from our menu of pre-designed experiments
  • Work with us to design a custom series aligned with grade levels and NGSS standards

Other Program Options

Beyond one-hour sessions, we also offer:

  • Extended in-class series (4-10 weeks, biology focus)
  • Summer camps with multi-day experiments
  • Large-scale Science Nights featuring 5-6 different labs in one evening
