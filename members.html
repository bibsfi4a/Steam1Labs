Meet Our Board Members

Our dedicated board members are passionate about STEM education and committed to our mission. Learn more about the individuals leading STEAMLabs.

Anna Han

International President

Hi! I'm Anna, a rising senior from Portland, Oregon and also the founder & current International President of STEAM Labs. I lead the operations and logistics that happen within STEAM Labs, helping to make our global programs possible. During my primary education, I rarely got experiences with hands-on STEM activities. This made it hard for me to later find my deep passion for science and engineering. Now, I'm honored to lead STEAM Labs and be able to bring a valuable experience to students all over the world.

Daniel Zhang

U.S. National President

Hey everyone! I'm Daniel Zhang, and I'm the U.S. National President of STEAM Labs! I'm a rising junior from sunny San Diego, California, and I also co-lead the Communications Team with Daniel Suh, where we meet with current chapter leaders and train new ones! Outside of STEAM Labs, I love playing piano, running, hitting the court for some badminton, and getting lost in a good book :)

Joseph Liu

Materials & Organization

Hi! I'm Joseph Liu, a rising senior from Austin, Texas, and I'm grateful to be the Materials & Organization group leader. Here, we help make documents to streamline the processes of individual teams, as well as the materials/procedure documents! Outside this, I enjoy cross-country running, playing my PS5, reading good-ol' classics, and doing other research.

Jenny Wu

Experimentation & Development

Hi! I'm Jenny, and I'm a sophomore from Houston, TX. As the experimentation group leader, I work with others to develop experiment procedures, finalize materials lists, and standardize measurements across chapters while helping brainstorm new experiments!

Hannah Oh

Illustration & Design

Hello! My name's Hannah Oh I am a rising Senior from Sunset High School in Portland, OR. I am the national leader of the Illustration Team, where we work to make children's coloring books and other creative pieces that bring kids closer to STEM education!

Sophia Lee

Inductions & Training

Hello! I am Sophia Lee, and I am a rising Senior from Portland, Oregon. As a leader of the inductions team, Kacey and I work with new members to make sure everyone is well prepared to help spread STEM education worldwide!

Kacey Song

Inductions & Training

Hello! I'm Kacey Song, and I am an upcoming junior from Coppell, Texas. I am very driven to help spread the wonders of STEM education to underserved communities worldwide, and being on the induction team with Sophia will ensure we execute our goal!
